About Us

NaaB Clan is a Persian gaming clan founded in 2013 by MH11.
The clan first started its activity in the game CoD:MW3 under the name 360 Team.
It then changed to other names including NuKe Clan and Black Rebels Family (BRF) and eventually became NaaB Clan.
After several years of gaming, we decided to temporarily disband the clan due to personal reasons, but the original members remained in touch and friends.
After several years of separation and being away from team activities, in 2022 we decided to get together and re-establish the clan and this time start our activity as a clan and its members on social media.
We are currently playing various games, but initially our main games were Call of Duty, Dota 2 and CS:GO.
Our goal in forming this clan is to get together and play in a friendly and intimate atmosphere along with growth and make progress.
We welcome talented content creators, skillful streamers, and professional players to join us.
